The blog for the ChatScrypt project! Initiated in 2019, by a two non-expert but technically-educated developers, as a part-time late-night hobby (to give an idea about the rate of progress). Goals for the project:

  • A chat client featuring....
  • zero-trust security, encryption, and transparency,
  • over a user-friendly searchable chat transcript;
  • speed and simplicity,
  • over a highly customizable platform;
  • portability across multiple devices and platforms.
Chatscrypt = chat + encryption + transcription.
